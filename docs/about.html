About

Sharing my passion for Magic The Gathering

Hello everybody! Let me introduce myself.

My name is Ludovic Heyberger, and I've been playing Magic The Gathering since 1995.

I discovered the game with the 4th Edition, HomeLands and Ice Age and I've been playing until Tempest.

I started playing again 5 years later with Onslaught, Legion and Sourge, and I never stopped since then.

I discovered Commander a few years ago and I got immediately totally addicted.

This website is my humble attempt at contributing to this wonderful format.

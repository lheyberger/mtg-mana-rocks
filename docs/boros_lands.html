Best Boros lands

Lands you should include in your Boros Commander deck, sorted by priority

Building a 2 colors Commander deck is always tricky. Compared to the other configurations (1 color and 3+ colors), 2 colors decks have less ways of fixing their manabase than other combinations.

On top of that, Boros is not exactly known to be good at mana fixing and drawing.

So it's particularly important to know what your options are if you want to make the most out of your manabase.

8. Inspiring Vantage

Check-land that will provide you the right mana the turn it enters the battlefield provided that you play it in your first three turns. After that, it's a bit slower. Pretty nice in low CMC Commander decks or otherwise in high CMC Commander deck. I wouldn't play it if most of my spells or big turns are around 4 CMC.

7. Clifftop Retreat

Check-land that will provide you the right mana from your second turn until the end of the game.

6. Rugged Prairie

Instant speed fixing for both of your colors if you happen to already have one of those two colors. Can provide you colorless mana in the meantime.

5. Battlefield Forge

Instant speed fixing for your Boros Commander with a 1 life drawback each time. It won't hurt you if you don't need the colored mana.

4. Sunbaked Canyon

Instant speed fixing for your Boros Commander with a 1 life drawback each time. You can replace itself during longer games.

3. Sacred Foundry

Almost as good as it's older brother, Plateau.

Prefered target when cracking an Arid Mesa if you have no relevant play to follow.

That's also why you should always wait until the last time to crack your fetch land: if you need to play something at instant speed like a Swords to Plowshares, just go fetch a Plateau, otherwise, at the end of your opponent's turn, go fetch a Sacred Foundry and let it enter the battlefield tapped.

2. Plateau

The king of dual lands: Perfect, no drawback, no tempo loss.

I only ranked Arid Mesa higher because of the low life point drawback to crack it and the added flexibility to shuffle the library of be recurred if needed.

1. Arid Mesa

This is the perfect land for your Boros Commander deck.

It's a Mountain when you need it. It's a Plains when you need it. If your budget allows it, it can be a Plateau at instant speed. If you don't have a relevant play to follow up, it can be a Sacred Foundry that enters the battlefield tapped.

Cracking a fetch land and shuffling your library is also what makes it so good. If for whatever reason, you're not happy with your scry, or cards you put back on top of your library with a Scroll Rack, you can get rid of them by cracking the Arid Mesa.

Fetch lands also enable some recursion shenanigans with cards like Brought Back or Sun Titan.

You can also double your fetch land activations with cards like Rings of Brighthearth.
